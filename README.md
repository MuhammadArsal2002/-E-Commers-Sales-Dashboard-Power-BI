📊 Overview

This project showcases an interactive E-Commerce Dashboard built in Power BI to analyze business performance across multiple quarters. The dashboard provides comprehensive insights into sales, profit, customer behavior, and regional performance, enabling better decision-making and strategy planning.

It combines clean data visualization, DAX-powered calculations, and user-friendly filters to create a professional, dynamic reporting experience.

⚙️ Key Features

⦁	📈 Sales & Profit Analysis: Track total sales, profit margins, and top-performing products.
⦁	👥 Customer Insights: Understand customer distribution, repeat purchases, and growth trends.
⦁	🌍 Regional Performance: Visualize area-wise sales performance with interactive maps and charts.
⦁	🕒 Time-Based Trends: Compare monthly and quarterly results to evaluate performance over time.
⦁	🧮 DAX Calculations: Used for creating KPIs like revenue growth, profit margin, and conversion rate.
⦁	🎯 Dynamic Filters: Filter data by region, product category, and time period.
⦁	☁️ Power BI Service Integration: Published online for real-time access and business reporting.

💡 Use Cases

⦁	Business managers can monitor sales and profits across multiple regions.
⦁	Marketing teams can identify high-performing products and customer trends.
⦁	Analysts can explore seasonal patterns and quarterly comparisons to forecast growth.
⦁	Decision-makers can use the dashboard to optimize strategy and resource allocation.
